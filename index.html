About Sharks Tank Pakistan

Transforming the entrepreneurial landscape of Pakistan

Sharks Tank Pakistan is a groundbreaking television series and business platform that brings together aspiring entrepreneurs with seasoned investors. The show provides a unique opportunity for Pakistani entrepreneurs to pitch their business ideas to a panel of wealthy investors, known as "Sharks," in hopes of securing investment funds and valuable mentorship.

Our mission is to foster entrepreneurship, innovation, and economic growth in Pakistan by providing a platform where brilliant business minds can connect with investors who have the resources and expertise to help them succeed.

Our Mission

To empower Pakistani entrepreneurs by providing access to capital, mentorship, and national exposure.

Our Vision

To become Pakistan's premier platform for entrepreneurial development and investment opportunities.

Our Values

Integrity, innovation, empowerment, and collaboration drive everything we do.

Program Format

How Sharks Tank Pakistan Works

1

Application

Entrepreneurs submit their applications online with their business details and pitch deck.

2

Screening

Our team reviews all applications and shortlists the most promising candidates.

3

Audition

Shortlisted entrepreneurs present their ideas to our producers in person or virtually.

4

Taping

Selected entrepreneurs pitch their businesses to the Sharks in front of cameras.

5

Investment

Sharks make offers, and entrepreneurs choose the best deal for their business.

6

After Show

Legal documentation and fund transfer, followed by mentorship and growth support.

Television Broadcast

The show airs on major Pakistani television networks, giving entrepreneurs national exposure.

Panel of Sharks

Our Sharks are successful entrepreneurs and investors with diverse industry expertise.

Legal Process

All investments go through proper legal documentation to protect both entrepreneurs and investors.
