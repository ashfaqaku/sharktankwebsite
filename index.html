About Sharks Tank Pakistan

Transforming Ideas into Successful Businesses

Sharks Tank Pakistan brings together the country's most brilliant entrepreneurs and most prominent investors in a high-stakes battle of business acumen.

Our platform provides a unique opportunity for Pakistani startups to secure the funding they need while gaining invaluable mentorship from experienced business leaders.

Since our inception, we've helped fund over 50 businesses across various sectors, creating thousands of jobs and contributing significantly to Pakistan's economic growth.
